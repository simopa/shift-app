Privacy Policy

Shift — Track how you feel before & after

Your data stays yours

Shift stores all your data locally on your device and, if you have iCloud enabled, in your private iCloud account. We do not have access to your data, and it never leaves your device or your personal iCloud storage.

No data collection

Shift does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We do not use analytics, tracking, advertising, or any third-party services that collect your data.

No accounts required

Shift does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use the app.

iCloud sync

If iCloud is enabled on your device, Shift uses Apple's CloudKit to sync your data across your devices. This data is stored in your private iCloud container and is protected by Apple's security and privacy measures. We cannot access this data.

Changes to this policy

If we ever update this policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date below.
