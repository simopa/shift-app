Frequently Asked Questions

How does Shift work?

Rate how you feel across 5 dimensions (energy, body tension, mood, physical comfort, mental clarity) using simple sliders. Do your practice, then rate again. Shift shows you the difference.

Is my data private?

Yes. All data stays on your device and your private iCloud account. We have no servers, no accounts, no analytics. We never see your data.

Can I add my own practices?

Yes. Shift comes with 6 default practices (walking, workout, yoga, meditation, breathwork, cold plunge), but you can add, edit, or remove any of them.

Does Shift sync across devices?

Yes, via your private iCloud. Your data syncs automatically between your iPhone devices signed into the same Apple ID.

Is there a subscription?

No. Shift is a one-time purchase. No subscriptions, no in-app purchases, no premium tiers.
